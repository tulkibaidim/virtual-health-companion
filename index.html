Quick First Aid Tips
❤️ CPR Steps
1. Call emergency services
2. Place hands on center of chest
3. Push hard and fast (100-120 compressions/min)
4. Give 2 rescue breaths every 30 compressions
5. Continue until help arrives
🩸 Bleeding Control
1. Apply direct pressure with clean cloth
2. Keep pressing for at least 10 minutes
3. Elevate the injured area if possible
4. Do not remove the cloth — add more on top
5. Call emergency if bleeding is severe
🔥 Burns Treatment
1. Cool under running water for 10-20 min
2. Do NOT use ice or butter
3. Cover loosely with sterile bandage
4. Take painkillers if needed
5. Seek medical help for large/deep burns
🫀 Heart Attack Signs
Signs: Chest pain, arm/jaw pain, shortness of breath, sweating, nausea

Actions:
1. Call 103 or 112 immediately
2. Have person sit/lie down comfortably
3. Loosen tight clothing
4. If trained, begin CPR if needed
